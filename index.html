About Me

My name is Bikini the Cat. Children are mother's greatest hits. Aren't they? ;-)) As you can tell from my profile picture, I've led a rather storied life with some very proud moments and others not so much. I am now happily retired from pet modeling and trying to lead a healthier life style away from spotlights and sins. If I had a wish--which isn't so much in my humble opinion, I am currently seeking an available bachelor cat to spend my golden years with. Please--no strays or rabid cats. Have a blessed day.
